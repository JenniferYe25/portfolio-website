Magic Leap Mixed Reality Educational Music Website

Intended to be a tool to aid early musicians in elementary school in picking out their very first instruments. It uses a variety of methods, employing point-and-click musical snippets and models on the Magic Leap platform.
Initially, I planned to input 360-degree video of my high school band's rehearsal to allow users to experience what it's like to be sitting in the middle of a symphonic band. However, during the creation process, this incorporation of such a function was not supported and was scrapped.
